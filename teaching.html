Teaching Experience

I have served as a Teaching Assistant for the following courses at UC Davis:

  • Calculus I (Fall 2022)
  • Calculus I for Biology and Medicine (Fall 2023, Winter 2024)
  • Calculus II for Biology and Medicine (Winter 2023, Spring 2023, Fall 2024, Winter 2025)
  • Population Dynamics & Estimation in the Wildlife, Fish, & Conservation Biology Department (Spring 2024, Spring 2025)

Mentoring Experience

I am a mentor in the UC Davis Mathematics Department's Directed Reading Program. In this role, I guide undergraduate students through independent studies on various topics, including differential equations in biomedical devices. Through weekly meetings, I facilitate reading of advanced mathematical texts, lead problem-solving discussions, and help students develop mathematical modeling skills beyond the standard curriculum. At the end of each quarter, students present their work at a program-wide conference.
